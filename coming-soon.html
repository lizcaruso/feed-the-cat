About

Let's Feed the Cat is a simple web game where you can feed a stray cat. As you feed the cat, the cat's expression will change and the site will count how many times you fed the cat.

It was designed and developed by cat mom to Pesto and Ravioli (and overall cat enthusiast) Liz Caruso for the final project in General Assembly's Front End Development Part-Time Course.

It utilizes a combination of skills developed in this course, ranging from HTML, responsive CSS Styling, and Javascript interactivity.

Illustrations are by Cute Little Workshop.
