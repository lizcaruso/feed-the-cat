About

Let's Feed the Cat is a simple web-based game where you can feed a stray cat.

It was designed and developed by cat mom to Pesto and Ravioli (and overall cat enthusiast) Liz Caruso for the final project in General Assembly's Front End Development Part-Time Course.

It utilizes a combination of skills developed in this course, ranging from HTML, responsive CSS Styling, and Javascript.

Illustrations are by Cute Little Workshop, and you can find their work on Creative Market or at their personal website.
